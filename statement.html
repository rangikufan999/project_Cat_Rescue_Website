Mission Statement

Our passion is making the lives of millions of cats whom have lost their home or have never had the luxury of having a home find dedicated owners willing to take care of them. Within our organization each member shares an uncanny passion towards cats and utilizes that commitment in striving towards doing the absolute best we can to ensure that every cat we come across finds its one true home with a family that will love it 100 percent. We are a non-profit organization that take donations for homeless cats all over the country, and can promise that every last penny goes toward making the lives of cats so much better.
