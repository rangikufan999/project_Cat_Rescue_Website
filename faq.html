Frequently Asked Questions

What kind of cats do you save? Are their any excluded?


No, we do not discriminate against any kinds of cats. Old, Young, Black, Tabby, we help all kinds of cats across the country. So rest assured, even black little kitties will find a good home!


How much of the donations "actually" go to help the cats?


The foundation's expenses are entirely out of the pockets of volunteers which leaves 100 percent of all donations made to the foundation going to the cats who desperately need it the most.


I've got information on a cat that could use your help, how can I get in touch?


You can get in touch with us by visiting our contact page and using the phone number to call us or sending us an email via the email link feline_rescue.mail@gmail.com. Alternatively, you can also reach out to one of our various social media accounts!
